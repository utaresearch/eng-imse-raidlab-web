Our Mission

RAID Lab's focus is to expand the uses of Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) in all industries. From healthcare to construction, RAID Labs is currently experimenting in a wide range of in-depth experiments to help further the uses of RFID to improve organization in all forms of industries and businesses.

Who We Are

Dr. Erick C. Jones, in an effort to support the marriage of industries' supply chain needs for automatic identification technology with academia's theoretical applications, has created an industry / academia collaboration. This facility's purpose is to support project initiatives like Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Logistics (Supply Chain Engineering), Manufacturing (Six-Sigma and Lean initiatives), and Information Technology (ERP, WMS). The facility's goal is to enhance the Industrial Engineering field by utilizing some of research methodologies to provide solutions in the areas of RFID, Supply Chain Logistics, and Engineering Management.
