RFID in the Construction Industry has been brought to the attention of the Radio Frequency and Supply Chain Logistics Lab at the University of Nebraska - Lincoln by the Mechanical Contractors Eduction Research Fund (MCERF). The Waldinger Corporation has generously agreed to the support the project through the expertise of its managers and use of job sites. The goals listed for the project include:
  • Security & tracking of onsite gas bottles.
  • Percent complete tracking.
