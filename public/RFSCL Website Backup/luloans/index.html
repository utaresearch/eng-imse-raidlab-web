Loans Unlimited, "We say "Yes" when others say "No".

Loans Unlimited is a company that has been around since 1997, and has built the business by helping all customers including clients with not so great credit. We can not always help everyone but we do find solutions for clients when they have been turned down by other lending organizations. We value our clients and treat them as clients not loans because we have survived in this business from referals from our clients and not mass marketing from media outlets. Loans Unlimited is a mortgage company that finds the best rates possible for its valued customers. By having flexible mobile loan officers we keep our overhead low so that we can pass our savings on to our clients. Our main office and processing center is in Houston. We have loan officers all over Texas, including Dallas, Tyler, San Antonio, and Austin. We are always looking for good personnel.
