Sourcing is entire set of business processes required to purchase goods and services. This includes selection of suppliers, design of supplier contracts, product design collaboration, procurement of material, and evaluation of supplier performance. Procurement is the process by which companies acquire raw materials, components, products, services and resources from suppliers to execute their operations.

Components of e-Procurement

The Sourcing Steps that should to be performed when creating an e-Procurement System include:

  • Supplier Scoring and Assessment
  • Supplier Selection and Contract negotiation
  • Design Collaboration
  • Procurement
  • Sourcing Planning and Evaluation
